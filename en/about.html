About WAGL
WAGL, Politics Startup
We-All-Govern Lab
WAGL is a democracy lab dedicated to transforming
the political status quo and ushering a better kind
of democracy. We capitalize on the collective intelligence
of the crowd, which we believe should be the
principal agent of change in this century.

Your Voice for All
WAGL believes that the voice of the citizens offer
our best chance of meaningful political change.
Our logo - the quotation mark - refers not only to
the voice of the citizen, but also to our vision
to become a vessel for those voices. In the same time,
the quotation mark signifies WAGL’s mission, which is
to quote the the clamor and dynamism of the masses
in the established politcial arena.
