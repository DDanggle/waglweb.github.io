WAGL studies and provides digital platforms on which everyday technology and democracy can mingle, increasing the breadth and depth of engagement from ordinary people. These platforms by demonstrating political utility firsthand, can widen the constituency of an A.R.T. Democracy.
P!NG KOREA
WAGL incubated P!NG Korea, the team that developed its eponymous Voter Advice Application. The first of its kind in the country, the application visualizes the level of agreement between a user and political parties on four political dimensions. P!NG Korea achieves this by canvassing 20 questions on topics such as the economy, civil liberties, North Korea, and the envrionment.
