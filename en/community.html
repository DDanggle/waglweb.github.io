Youth Political
Participation Project
WAGL is developing an online forum for young people that will help young people voice their opinion on issues specific to their generation.
WAGL’s forum will enable young people to exchange their opinion and give them a place to be heard. Such a forum provides the opportunity for young people to position themselves as full members of our political community.
