Manifesto of WAGL
Why WAGL?
시민이 주인공이 되는 정치
언젠가부터 정치는 깡통 통조림처럼
획일적인 맛이 되어버렸습니다.
Around many places, politics has become an exchange of canned platitudes and abuses, monopolized by professional politicians. Professional politicians are removed from everyday life, only craving the applause of an audience, not the bustle of the citizenry. To them, these ordinary voices amount only to cacophonous complaints, irrelevant to their electoral prospects.

In our rapidly changing world, a vote every four, five years is too long a wait for a critical update to our political system. Millions of clients are accessing a server that is chronically overloaded and too slow to adapt. Political alienation and democratic regression all tax the citizen dearly. The advent of the internet age, with its mobile devices, social media, instant messaging, and collaborative document sharing, have all fundamentally transformed our breadth and depth of interpersonal relationships. Each person is but a drop, but these tools of the modern age can funnel us into an ocean of collective intelligence - retrieving the professionalized political sphere into the hands of the citizens.
How?
“엄지”로 만나는 일상정치
Is WAGL doing politics? No.
WAGL wants us all to do politics.
WAGL believes that a better, truer democratic politics creates a realm for civic exchange predicated upon the active participation of the citizen, ready to solve the challenges facing us all.

We can realize this vision of a participatory, deliberative democracy by exploiting the technological advances upon us. A more routine vision of politics - one in which we can debate policy over lunch, propose a new bill to parliament, score candidates after work around the dinner table, and electing those who have never seen th light of traditional media - is possible. This, and more, is possible with our thumbs.

WAGL believes that politics is possible anywhere and anytime, that politics is not a subject of disgust, but a gathering of citizens.
So what?
시민의 참여로
‘A.R.T. 민주주의’를 만들어갑니다
와글은 특권화, 직업화된 기성정치의 틀을 뛰어넘어
정치의 본래적 의미를 찾기 위한 모든 풀뿌리차원의
정치혁신을 연구하고 지원합니다.
WAGL studies and supports all grassroots-based political innovations that try to transcend the traits of traditional politics - elitism, professionalism, and traditionalism.

WAGL is especially interested in direct citizen participation in politics through the internet and social media, flat decision-making models based on collective intelligence, as well as the political organization of grassroots groups. We plan and execute various projects with individuals and groups that have already implemented such endeavors. We are eager to connect with anyone interested in a better democracy, dedicated to bringing citizens back into mainstream politics.
