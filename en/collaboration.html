WAGL creates a collaborative environment for social innovation by bringing together innovators in Camps and Lecture Series.
We not only share experiences and ideas from pioneers that channel the voice of the crowd in politics, grass roots activism, civic tech, and journalism, but also share the broad stream of social innovation in Korea and around the world.
International Meet-up,
Camp: by the Crowd '15
30 social innovators from around the world convened in Jeju, Korea.
The participants, pooling their wisdom to gather the “power of the crowd”, spent three nights and four days in an unconference that was full of sharing know hows and identifying fault lines. WAGL continues to communicate with these innovators, probing for opportunities for collaboration in the future.
2015 WAGL Lecture Series
WAGL hosted two lectures where innovators from the Camp were given the chance to share their thoughts with the Korean public.
The 2015 Lecture Series mainly focused on political innovators, and features several digital tools that allowed for instant mutual interaction between the speakers and the audience. International activists, programmers, and theorists imparted their experiences of including the 99% into their democratic processes onto a Korean audience thirsting for political innovation.
